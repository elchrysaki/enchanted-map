## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

NASA's Lucy Mission LEAP Internship offers participants the chance to develop leadership skills by managing and mentoring teams in the Mission Concept Academy. The internship is conducted online and focuses on space mission concept development, team management, and professional growth.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.lspace.asu.edu/mentor-internship)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

The L'SPACE Program, part of Arizona State University, organizes this internship to support NASA's Lucy Mission and foster student leadership in space-related projects.
