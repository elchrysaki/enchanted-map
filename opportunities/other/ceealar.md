## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

CEEALAR is a residential research community in Blackpool, UK, supporting researchers and builders working on existential risk reduction, effective altruism, AI safety, longtermism, and global catastrophic risk reduction. Residents benefit from subsidized accommodation, vegan food, and a collaborative environment.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.ceealar.org/apply-to-ceealar?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

CEEALAR is an organization dedicated to supporting research and projects in existential risk reduction and effective altruism cause areas. It operates a residential community for aligned researchers and builders.

## 👥 Who can apply

Applicants should be actively working on projects related to existential risk reduction, effective altruism, AI safety, longtermism, or global catastrophic risk reduction. No explicit nationality or residency restrictions are stated.

🚀 **[Open the official application page →](https://www.ceealar.org/apply-to-ceealar?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 📍 Where and how it happens

Residency is in-person at CEEALAR's facility in Blackpool, United Kingdom.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. Initial stays are up to 6 weeks, with the possibility to extend up to 12 months.

## 💸 Fees, funding and support

No application fee. Grantee residents receive subsidized accommodation and vegan food, with a sliding scale contribution (£0-900/month). Patron residents pay market-rate (£30+/night).

## 🛠️ What you will do

Residents engage in focused research and project work, participate in community activities, and collaborate with other researchers and changemakers in the field.

## 🚀 How to apply

Apply online via the official application page. Applicants should describe their project and its relevance to existential risk reduction or EA cause areas. Decisions are typically made within 7-14 days.
