## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

The NASA Lucy Mission Internship is a 10-week in-person program for students interested in space science and engineering. Interns gain hands-on experience and mentorship at collaborative sites in the United States, contributing to NASA mission projects.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.lspace.asu.edu/lucy-internship)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

The L'SPACE Program organizes this internship, connecting students with mentors at NASA and partner organizations.
