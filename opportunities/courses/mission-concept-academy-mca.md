## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

Mission Concept Academy (MCA) is a free, online technical training program for students interested in NASA and space careers. Participants gain hands-on experience in mission planning, proposal writing, and technical skills through interactive sessions.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.lspace.asu.edu/mission-concept-academy)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

L'SPACE is a US-based organization offering educational programs funded by NASA, including the Mission Concept Academy.

## 👥 Who can apply

Undergraduate and graduate students enrolled at US colleges and universities are eligible. The program is conducted in English.

## 📍 Where and how it happens

The MCA is delivered online and hosted from the United States. No travel or relocation is required.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

Application deadline: 2 August 2026, 11:59 PM PT. Start and end dates are not confirmed.

## 💸 Fees, funding and support

Participation is free. No application fee, travel, accommodation, or meal support is needed. No stipend or salary is provided.

## 🛠️ What you will do

Participants engage in mission planning, proposal writing, technical training, and interactive online sessions. Networking opportunities with NASA professionals are included.

## 🚀 How to apply

Applications are submitted through the official MCA program page. No application fee is required. Specific document requirements are not confirmed.
