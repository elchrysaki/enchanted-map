## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

EA Summit: Wellington brings together individuals interested in effective altruism for a one-day conference featuring talks, sessions, and networking. Participants will explore topics such as global health, animal welfare, AI safety, and effective giving.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/ea-summit-wellington?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

The event is organized by Effective Altruism (EA Global), a network focused on using evidence and reason to tackle the world's most pressing problems.

## 👥 Who can apply

Open to people from across Aotearoa New Zealand who are interested in effective altruism. Both newcomers and those already involved in the community are encouraged to apply.

## 📍 Where and how it happens

The summit takes place in person at Tiakiwai Conference Centre, Wellington, New Zealand.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

The event is scheduled for 19 September 2026. No application deadline is confirmed; check the official page for updates.

## 💸 Fees, funding and support

No information is available regarding application fees, participation fees, scholarships, or travel support.

## 🛠️ What you will do

Attendees will join talks and sessions on global challenges, network with others interested in effective altruism, and learn from practitioners in the field.
