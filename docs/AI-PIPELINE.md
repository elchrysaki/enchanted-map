# 🤖 The Enchanted Map AI Pipeline

The Enchanted Map uses AI to research, organize, and present student opportunities while keeping publication under human control.

AI may assist with preparation, but it may not approve, merge, or publish an opportunity by itself.

---

## 🗺️ Pipeline Overview

```text
Contributor submits a GitHub Issue
            ↓
1. Original submission is preserved
            ↓
2. Official sources are researched
            ↓
3. A structured researched copy is created
            ↓
4. A readable quest draft is produced
            ↓
5. A moderator review report is generated
            ↓
6. A draft pull request is opened
            ↓
7. A human moderator reviews and edits
            ↓
8. The moderator merges or rejects the draft
            ↓
9. Merged quests are marked as published
            ↓
10. Public indexes and clickable lists rebuild
