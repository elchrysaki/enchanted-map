# 🧭 EA Summit: Istanbul

**Effective Altruism Turkey**

🎯 **Conference** · 💻 **In Person** · 📍 **In-person at HAN Spaces Etiler, Istanbul, Turkey**

The event brings together individuals interested in effective altruism for talks, discussions, and networking. Participants can connect with the EA community, learn about impactful causes, and explore ways to make a greater difference.

## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

EA Summit: Istanbul is a conference designed for both newcomers and experienced individuals interested in effective altruism. The event features talks, discussions, and networking, focusing on impactful causes and strategies for making a difference.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/ea-summit-istanbul?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

Effective Altruism Turkey is the local chapter organizing the summit, aiming to foster community and discussion around effective altruism in Turkey.

## 👥 Who can apply

Anyone interested in effective altruism is welcome to attend. No specific eligibility criteria regarding nationality, academic level, or age have been announced.

## 📍 Where and how it happens

The summit will take place in person at HAN Spaces Etiler, Istanbul, Turkey.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

The event is scheduled for 24 October 2026. Application or registration deadlines have not yet been announced.

## 💸 Fees, funding and support

No information about fees, scholarships, or travel support is currently available. Check the official page for updates.

## 🛠️ What you will do

Participants will attend talks, join discussions, and network with others interested in effective altruism. Topics include global poverty, animal welfare, AI risks, and more.

## 🚀 How to apply

Registration details will be provided closer to the event date. Monitor the official page for announcements.
