# 🧭 Syntony Engineering Fellowship

**Syntony Engineering**

🎯 **Fellowship** · 💻 **Online** · 📍 **Remote (hosted by Syntony Engineering, Durham, NC, United States)**

**[🌐 Official page](https://www.syntonyresearch.ai/work/engineering-fellowship/?utm_source=ea-opps)**

> [!TIP]
> The Syntony Engineering Fellowship is a remote technical fellowship for exceptional engineers, researchers, and builders interested in AI evaluation, safety, and governance. Fellows collaborate directly with Syntony's founder on impactful projects, gaining hands-on experience in company-building and technical leadership. Applications are open with no specified deadline.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Application deadline:** Rolling applications  
> **Program dates:** Not confirmed  
> **Location:** Remote (hosted by Syntony Engineering, Durham, NC, United States)  
> **Format:** Online

---

## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

A remote technical fellowship for engineers, researchers, and builders to work on AI evaluation, safety, and governance projects with Syntony Engineering.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.syntonyresearch.ai/work/engineering-fellowship/?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

Syntony Engineering is a US-based company focused on AI evaluation, safety, and governance.

## 👥 Who can apply

Applicants should demonstrate strong programming skills (Python or TypeScript), independent building experience, clear written communication, and an interest in AI safety, evaluation, or governance. No explicit nationality, residency, or age requirements are specified.

## 📍 Where and how it happens

The fellowship is remote, hosted by Syntony Engineering in Durham, NC, United States.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis; no deadline is specified.

## 💸 Fees, funding and support

There are no application or participation fees. The fellowship does not provide a stipend or salary, but exceptional contributors may be considered for future full-time roles. As the program is remote, travel, accommodation, and meals are not applicable.

## 🛠️ What you will do

Fellows collaborate directly with Syntony's founder on projects related to AI evaluation infrastructure, agent systems, risk data, and research tooling, gaining hands-on experience in technical prioritization and company-building.

## 🚀 How to apply

Applicants should submit evidence of technical work and programming ability. The selection process includes async and technical conversations, a scoped project discussion or work sample, and a written project scope for invited fellows.

- **[🌐 Explore the official page](https://www.syntonyresearch.ai/work/engineering-fellowship/?utm_source=ea-opps)**
