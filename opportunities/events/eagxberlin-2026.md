# 🧭 EAGxBerlin 2026

**Effective Altruism Global**

🎯 **Conference** · 💻 **In Person** · 📍 **In-person in Berlin, Germany**

**[🌐 Official page](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/eagxberlin-2026?utm_source=ea-opps)**

> [!TIP]

Participants can attend workshops, talks, and networking sessions focused on global challenges and high-impact careers. The event is designed for those already familiar with effective altruism.

> **Application deadline:** Applications not yet open  
> **Program dates:** 13 November 2026 to 15 November 2026  
> **Location:** In-person in Berlin, Germany

## ✨ Why this opportunity is worth your attention

EAGxBerlin 2026 is a conference for the effective altruism community, offering workshops, talks, and networking opportunities in Berlin, Germany. The event focuses on global challenges and high-impact careers.

🌐 **[Visit the official opportunity website →](https://www.effectivealtruism.org/ea-global/events/eagxberlin-2026?utm_source=ea-opps)**

## 🏛️ Meet the organizer

Effective Altruism Global organizes EAGxBerlin 2026, aiming to connect and strengthen the European effective altruism community.

## 👥 Who can apply

The event is primarily for those already familiar with effective altruism, especially members of the European, Western, and Central European EA community. Some prior knowledge of effective altruism is recommended.

## 📍 Where and how it happens

EAGxBerlin 2026 will take place in-person in Berlin, Germany.

## 🗓️ Dates to remember

The conference runs from 13 to 15 November 2026. Applications are not yet open; check the official page for updates.

## 🛠️ What you will do

Participants will attend workshops, talks, discussions, and networking sessions focused on effective altruism, global challenges, and high-impact careers.

## 🚀 How to apply

Applications are not yet open. Visit the official event page for future updates and instructions.
